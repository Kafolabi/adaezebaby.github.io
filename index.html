How to make Spagetti Jollof

INGREDIENT

  • Sphagetti

  • Tomato

  • Pepper

  • Curry,Pepper,Thyme leaves and salt

  • Chicken

    • Procedure
    1. Boil water in a pot
    2. Add Sphagetti and boil for a while(15-30mins)
    3. While the spaghetti is boiling,let,s start making the sauce
    4. Wash the tomato and pepper and Garlic.Remove the garlic pills
    5. Blend the tomato,Pepper and Garlic in a blender or you can grind it,but it is preferable to blend for a smoother and taster look.
    6. Wash the chicken until it is clean,then put the chicken in a pot,add water and wait for 45 to 90mins(Matters on amout of chicken,if the chicken is not yet ready wait for a longer amout of time)
    7. Get a separate pot and pour the blended tomato and pepper into the pot.
    8. Spice with Curry,Pepper,Tyme and salt-You can add any other spice of your choice
    9. Now let's turn on the gas
    10. Fry the sauce and add the garlic cloves,1 tablespoon salt,water,cuurry powder and rosemary Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Then remove the stew from heat Now let's come back to the chicken.Check the chicken whether it is done by making sure the chicken is soft and tasty as you want it. When you are sure the chicken is ready off the burner.

      The finishing procedure

      Now pour the chicken into the stew and turn Leave it for sometime to boil so that the chicken flavor can enter the stew(This process will take 10mins) This is the last process for the Spaghetti Jollof Now pour the Stew into the Spaghetti and mix The Spaghetti Jollof is ready.

      Enjoy!!!
